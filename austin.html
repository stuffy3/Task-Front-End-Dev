About Austin

The city of Austin has a population of 979,882. The city was incorporated on December 27th, 1839. Austin is fairly close to being located in the middle of the state, just a bit south-east. The city of Austin is classified as urban. The average household income is $91,000, higher than the median of the whole state of Texas which is $75,780.
